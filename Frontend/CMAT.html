Tips


  • Prioritize questions you are confident in and keep track of time. Aim to complete sections in the given time limit.
  • Stay updated with current affairs, business news, and general knowledge. Read newspapers and magazines regularly.
  • Practice puzzles, seating arrangements, and logical sequences. Developing a systematic approach to these questions is essential.
  • Strengthen your basics in arithmetic, algebra, and geometry. Practice solving problems quickly and accurately.

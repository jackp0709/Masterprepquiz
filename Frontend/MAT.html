Tips

  • Improve your vocabulary by reading and learning new words daily. This will help in the Language Comprehension section.
  • Stay informed about current events, business news, and economic developments, both in India and globally.
  • Work on different types of reasoning questions, including analytical reasoning, to enhance your logical thinking.
  • Practice interpreting graphs, charts, and tables quickly and accurately. Speed and precision are key in this section.
